Strategy & Math Details

The bot employs a multi-indicator confirmation strategy. A BUY or SELL signal is only triggered when at least 2 out of the 3 indicators align.

1. Relative Strength Index (RSI)

RSI is a momentum oscillator that measures the speed and change of price movements. The bot uses RSI to identify overbought or oversold conditions.

  • Math: RSI = 100 - [100 / (1 + (Avg Gain / Avg Loss))]
  • Buy Condition: RSI < 40 (Approaching Oversold)
  • Sell Condition: RSI > 60 (Approaching Overbought)
2. MACD

Moving Average Convergence Divergence measures the relationship between two moving averages (Fast and Slow) of a price.

  • Math: MACD Line = EMA(Fast) - EMA(Slow)
    Signal Line = EMA(MACD Line)
  • Buy Condition: MACD crosses above the Signal Line.
  • Sell Condition: MACD crosses below the Signal Line.
3. Bollinger Bands

A volatility indicator consisting of a moving average (Middle Band) and upper/lower bands defined by standard deviations.

  • Math: Middle = SMA(Period)
    Upper = Middle + (StdDev * Period)
    Lower = Middle - (StdDev * Period)
  • Buy Condition: Price touches or falls below Lower Band.
  • Sell Condition: Price touches or rises above Upper Band.

Leveraged Margin Engine Details

This trading bot does not just trade spot assets; it supports Leveraged Margin Trading, amplifying both potential profits and risks.

  • LONG Position: The bot believes the asset price will rise. It uses the `Leverage` multiplier to borrow USDT, buys a large amount of the asset, and hopes to sell it later at a higher price.
  • SHORT Position: The bot believes the asset price will fall. It borrows the asset, sells it immediately for USDT, and hopes to buy it back later at a cheaper price to repay the loan.
  • Margin & Collateral: For a trade of $25 with 10x Leverage, the engine only locks $25 of your available cash (Initial Margin). The total trade position size is $250.
  • Unrealized PnL: The profit or loss is calculated on the full $250 size, but is added/subtracted from your $25 margin. This is why leveraged PnL percentages can exceed 100%.
  • Liquidation Price: If the market moves aggressively against the bot, the position will be automatically liquidated when the Margin is 95% depleted to protect the rest of the account balance from going negative.
